## **Banana Bread** {#banana-bread-1}

**Banana Bread Ingredients:**

Ripe Bananas (peeled) 2-3 medium (240-360g)  
Unsalted Butter: ½ cup (113g)  
Sugar: 1 cup (200g)  
Large Eggs (room temperature): 2  
Vanilla Extract: 1 tsp (4g)  
AP Flour: 2 cups (240g)  
Baking Soda: 1 tsp (4g)  
Salt: ½ tsp (2g)  
Sour Cream: ½ cup (120g)

**Tools Needed:**

Mixing Bowls, Measuring cups and spoons, Electric mixer/Whisks, 9 inch loaf pan, parchment paper, spatula or bowl scraper.
