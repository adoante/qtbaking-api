## **Simple Chocolate Chip Cookies**  {#simple-chocolate-chip-cookies-1}

**EZ PZ EVERYONE CAN MAKE THESE**

**Difficulty:⭐️**

**Ingredients:**  
1 cup butter softened (go put it on your counter the morning of or night before so its soft)  
1 cup white (granulated) sugar  
1 cup brown sugar  
2 tsp vanilla extract  
2 large eggs  
3 cups all-purpose flour  
1 tsp baking soda  
½ tsp baking powder  
1½ tsp salt  
1½ cups chocolate chips (or chunks, or chopped chocolate) \- you can also add 1 cup of chopped nuts if you want

**YOU WILL NEED:**  
Oven  
Mixing bowls  
Cookie Sheet   
Measuring Cups   
Mixer (if you have one)   
Parchment paper
