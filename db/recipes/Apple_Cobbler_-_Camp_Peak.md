## **Apple Cobbler \- Camp Peak** {#apple-cobbler---camp-peak}

**Fruit Filling Ingredients:**

Granny Smith Apples: 4 cups (800g)  
Lemon Juice: 1 tbsp (14g)  
Cinnamon: 2 tbsp (24g)  
Nutmeg: ¼ tsp (1g)  
Salt: ¼  tsp (1g)  
All-Purpose Flour: 1 tbsp (14g)  
Granulated Sugar: ½  cups (100g)  
Brown Sugar: ½ cup (100g)

**Cobbler Topping Ingredients:**

All-Purpose Flour: 1 cup (200g)  
Baking Powder: 1 tsp (4g)  
Baking Soda: ¼ tsp (1g)  
Salt: ½ tsp (2g)  
Unsalted Butter ¼ cup (50g)  
Milk: 6 tbsp (85g)

**Tools Needed:**  
Mixing Bowls  
Measuring cups and spoons  
Hand mixer or stand mixer  
Whisk  
Spatula/Bowl scraper  
Dutch Oven  
Knife  
Foil
