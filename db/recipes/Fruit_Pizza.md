## **Fruit Pizza** {#fruit-pizza}

**Sugar Cookie Crust Ingredients:**

AP Flour: 1 ½ cups (188g) spooned & leveled  
Cornstarch: 1 ½ teaspoon (4g)  
Baking Powder: 1 teaspoon (4g)  
Salt: 1/4 teaspoon (2g)  
Unsalted Butter: ½   cup (113g) **softened**  
Granulated Sugar: ¾ cup (150g)  
Large Egg: 1 **room temperature**  
Pure Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon (4g)

**Topping Ingredients:**

Cream Cheese (softened): 8oz (226g)  
Unsalted Butter (softened): 3 tablespoons (43g)  
Powdered Sugar: 1 ¼ cups (150g)  
Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon (4g)  
Sliced Fresh Fruit: 3 cups (400g)  
	Fruit Toppings-  
		Mandarin oranges, strawberries, blackberries, blueberries, raspberries, 		kiwis

**Tools Needed:**

Mixing Bowls, Measuring cups and spoons, Electric mixer/Whisks, baking sheets, parchment paper, spatula or bowl scraper, knife.
