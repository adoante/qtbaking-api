## **Pumpkin Scones** {#pumpkin-scones}

Soft, flaky, crumbly, and comforting 

**Difficulty Rating: ⭐️**

**Pumpkin Scone Ingredients:**

AP Flour: 2 cups (250g) spooned & leveled  
Baking Powder: 2 ½ teaspoons (10g)  
Ground Cinnamon: 1 teaspoon (2.6g)  
Pumpkin Pie Spice: 1 ½ teaspoon (3g)  
Salt: ½ teaspoon (3g)  
Unsalted Butter: ½   cup (113g) **frozen**  
Heavy Cream: ⅓ cup \+ 2 tablespoons (105ml), divided  
Large Egg: 1  
Canned Pumpkin Puree: ½ cup (115g) blotted  
Light Brown Sugar: ½ cup (100g)  
Pure Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon (5ml)  
Optional- Coarse Sugar for sprinkling before baking

**Maple Glaze Ingredients:**

Butter: 2 tablespoons (28g)  
Powdered Sugar: 1  cup (112g) sifted  
Pure Maple Syrup: ⅓ cup (80 ml)  
Salt, pinch to taste

**Tools Needed:**

Mixing Bowls, Measuring cups and spoons, Whisks, Box Grater, Pastry cutter or fork (for making crumbs), sharp knife, baking sheets, parchment paper or silicone baking mats, pastry brush, small saucepan, cooling rack
