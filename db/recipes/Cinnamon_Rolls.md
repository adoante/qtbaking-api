## **Cinnamon Rolls** {#cinnamon-rolls}

**Ingredients**  
1 Cup Milk   
3 Cubes Butter (each cube is half a cup)   
2 Tbsp Instant Yeast   
2 Cups \+ 1 Tbsp Sugar    
1 Cup Water   
3 Eggs   
6-7 Cups Flour (depends on humidity; usually it’s 6\)   
1 Tsp Salt   
2 Tbsp Ground Cinnamon   
1 Package Powdered Sugar   
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
