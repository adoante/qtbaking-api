## **Apple Streusel Muffins** {#apple-streusel-muffins}

This week, QT is making a sweet, crumbly, and delicious apple treat paired with a warm maple glaze\! 

**Difficulty Rating:** ⭐️½ (only because there’s streusel & a glaze)

**Muffin Ingredients:**

Granulated Sugar: 2 cups (400g)  
Vegetable Oil: 1 ½ cups (360 ml)  
Eggs: 3  
Vanilla Extract: 2 teaspoons (10ml)  
AP Flour: 3 cups (375g)  
Salt: 1 teaspoon (5g)  
Baking Soda: 1 teaspoon (5g)  
Granny Smith Apples: 2 cups (300g) finely chopped  
Ground Cinnamon: 2 teaspoons (5g)  
Optional Walnuts or Pecans: 1 cup (120g) chopped

**Streusel Topping Ingredients:**

Salted Butter: 6 tablespoons cold  
AP Flour: ⅔ cup  
Old Fashioned Oats: ½ cups   
Light Brown Sugar: ⅓  cup packed  
Ground Cinnamon: ½ teaspoon 

**Maple Glaze Ingredients:**

Butter: 4 tablespoons melted  
Powdered Sugar: ½  cup (60g) sifted  
Maple Syrup: 3 tablespoons   
A big pinch of salt to taste if using unsalted butter

**Tools Needed:**

Mixing Bowls, Measuring cups and spoons, Whisks, Spatula or fork (for making crumbs), muffin tins, baking cups, non stick spray, electric mixer or hand whisk, cookie scoop or large spoon (for portioning dough), cooling rack (optional)
