## **Chocolate Crinkle Cookies** {#chocolate-crinkle-cookies}

**Cookie Ingredients:**

Granulated Sugar: 2 cups (400g)  
Vegetable Oil: ¾ cup (180 ml)  
Cocoa Powder: ¾ cup (75g)  
Eggs: 4  
Vanilla Extract: 2 tsp (10 ml)  
All-Purpose Flour: 2 ½ cup (290g)  
Baking Powder: 2 tsp (10g)  
Salt: ½ tsp (3g)  
Powdered Sugar (for coating): 2 cups (240g)

**Tools Needed:**  
Mixing Bowls  
Measuring cups and spoons  
Hand mixer or stand mixer  
Whisk  
Plastic wrap  
Parchment Paper  
Spatula/Bowl scraper  
Baking Sheet  
Cookie scoop/spoon  
Cooling Rack
