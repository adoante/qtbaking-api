## **Clam Chowder** {#clam-chowder-1}

Clam Chowder Ingredients:

Clams: 2 cans  
Potatoes: 2  
Celery: 1 cup (100g)  
Onions: 1 cup (150g)  
Butter: 4 tbsp (57g)  
All Purpose Flour: 3/4 cups (95g)
Sugar: 1/2 tsp (2g)
Half and Half: 4 cups (960 g)

Tools Needed:
Measuring cups and spoons
Knife
Cutting Board
Pot
