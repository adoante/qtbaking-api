## **Lemon Bars** {#lemon-bars}

**Crust Ingredients:**

AP Flour: 2 ½ cups (300g)  
Powdered Sugar: ⅔ cup (66g)  
Salt: 1/2 teaspoon (4g)  
Unsalted Butter:12 tbsp (170g) melted

**Lemon Filling Ingredients:**

Lemon Zest: 3 tbsps (44g)  
AP Flour: ½ cup (60g)  
Sugar: 3 cups (600g)  
Large Egg (room temp): 8  
Lemon Juice: 1 cup (240mL)  
Powdered Sugar for dusting

**Tools Needed:** 

Mixing Bowls, Measuring cups and spoons, Food Processor (optional), 9x13 baking dish, parchment paper, spatula or bowl scraper, knife, zester, fruit juicer.
