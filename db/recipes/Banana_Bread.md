## **Banana Bread**  {#banana-bread}

**BUY THE RIPEST BROWNEST BANANAS NOW\* the riper the bananas the better so buy bananas *NOW*** 

**Difficulty: ⭐️** 

**Ingredients:**   
1/2 Cup Butter   
1 Cup Sugar   
2 or 3 Ripe Bananas   
2 eggs   
1 tsp vanilla extract   
2 cups Flour   
1 tsp baking Soda   
1/2 teaspoon Salt   
1/2 cup sour cream 

**Bonus:** if you like a crunchy top you can get some turbinado sugar to sprinkle on top 

**YOU WILL NEED:**  
9inch loaf pan OR if you don’t have that you can use a 9” round, 8”x8”, or a 9”x13. Of course it just will make it a different shape than a bread loaf but that’s okay
