## **Spudnuts** {#spudnuts}

**Ingredients-**  
2 Package Yeast  (14 g)
1 ¾ Cups Warm Milk  (420 g)
½ Cup Sugar  (100 g)
½ Cup Mashed Potatoes  (120 g)
2 Tsp Salt  (12 g)
2 Eggs 
½ Cup Warm Water  (120 g)
½ Cup Shortening  (95 g)
1 Tsp Baking Powder  (5 g)
1 Tsp Vanilla  (5 g)
6 \- 7 Cup Flour (750 - 875 g)

**Glaze**  
1lb Powdered Sugar  (454 g)
2 Tsp Margarine  (28 g)
1 Tsp Vanilla  (5 g)
Hot Water - add gradually

*Add enough hot water to make it thin enough to drop spudnuts in*  
*Let excess drain off.*

**Directions**  
1\. Soften yeast in warm water  
2\. Scald milk, add shortening, sugar, salt, mashed potatoes, eggs, vanilla and beat.  
3\. Add yeast and flour and make a dough too soft to knead.  
4\. Let raise double in size, roll out and cut  
5\. Let raise and cook in hot grease  
6\. Drain on paper towel and glaze
