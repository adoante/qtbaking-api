## **Chai Cake** {#chai-cake}

**Chai Cake Ingredients:**  
All Purpose Flour: 2 ¼ cups (282g)  
Cinnamon: 1 tbsp (8g)  
Ginger: 1 ½ tsp (3g)  
Allspice: ½ tsp (1g)  
Nutmeg: ½ tsp (1g)  
Cardamom: 1 tsp (2g)  
Ground Cloves: ¼ tsp (1g)  
Baking Powder: 1 ½ tsp (6g)  
Baking Soda: ¼ tsp (1g)  
Salt: ½ tsp (3g)  
Granulated White Sugar: 1 cup (200g)  
Brown Sugar (packed): ½ cup (110g)  
Eggs (room temp): 3  
Vanilla Extract: 1 tbsp (13g)  
Whole Milk (room temp): 1 cup (240g)  
Unsalted Butter (softened): 10 tbsp (140g)

**Chai Milk Soak:**  
Sweetened Condensed Milk: ½ cup (150g)  
Vanilla Extract: 1 tsp (4g)  
Chai Tea Bags: 2  
Whole Milk: ½ cup (120g)

**Brown Butter Cream Frosting:**  
Unsalted Butter: 1 ¼ cup (280g)  
Cream Cheese: 1 cup (226g)  
Powdered Sugar: 2 cups (260g)

**Tools Needed:**  
Mixing Bowls  
Measuring cups and spoons  
Hand mixer or stand mixer  
Cake Pan  
Parchment Paper  
Spatula/Bowl scraper  
Cooling Rack  
Sauce Pan
